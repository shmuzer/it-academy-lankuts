Cookies To enrich and perfect your online experience, Buzz Fit uses “Cookies”, similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer. A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Buzz Fit uses cookies to help Buzz Fit identify and track visitors, their usage of https://buzz.fit, and their website access preferences. Buzz Fit visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to disallow cookies before using Buzz Fit’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of Buzz Fit’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies. By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to Buzz Fit’s use of cookies.
